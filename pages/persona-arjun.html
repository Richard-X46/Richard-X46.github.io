In-Class Activity 2: Persona - Arjun

User Persona for Graduate Student Affordability Story

This persona represents Arjun, a graduate student facing financial hardship while pursuing his studies at an Ontario university. The persona helps humanize the data and provides a concrete character for our storytelling approach.

It includes demographic information, goals, frustrations, daily challenges, and the financial trade-offs Arjun makes on a weekly basis.
